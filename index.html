Project 01 / 02
Brazilian E-Commerce Strategic Market Analysis
Logistics · E-Commerce · Supply Chain · Predictive Modelling
  • Analyzed 100K+ orders by integrating 8+ relational tables using SQL and Python to investigate the relationship between freight costs and delivery efficiency.
  • Applied Scikit-learn regression models to forecast delivery lead times and identify key variables impacting order fulfillment speed across regions.
  • Uncovered a 45% delivery time gap between North/Northeast and Southern regions despite comparable freight costs — confirming infrastructure as the primary bottleneck over pricing.
  • Identified a High-Friction Zone in the North where freight cost and delivery time showed weak correlation r < 0.3, revealing "Express" shipping premiums rarely translated to faster delivery — recommended a decentralized warehouse strategy.
  • Designed a Tableau Storyboard with a What-If simulator, enabling stakeholders to benchmark carrier performance against regional averages and support logistics planning.
